Teacher Appreciation


What is the purpose of Teacher Appreciation Week?

Teacher's appreciation week is an opportunity to thank teachers and recognize the impact they have on the community. It's easy for some educators to feel undervalued, but one local teacher said the biggest reward is working with students.
